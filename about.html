About Me

I am a dedicated high school student with a strong focus on STEM, particularly aerospace engineering and space exploration, driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact in the field. My passion for technology and engineering is matched by a deep interest in research, which provides me with the opportunity to contribute new insights to the scientific community while furthering my own understanding.

Outside of academics, I am an active member of my high school tennis team and a regular at the gym. Running is another passion of mine, which allows me to stay fit and clear my mind. I am also deeply committed to my community, volunteering and mentoring with the Vallab Youth Organization (VYO), where I help younger kids stay connected to their cultural roots and foster a sense of inclusion. Additionally, I co-founded the Red Cross Club at my school, where we organize blood drives and lead informative sessions on first aid, empowering others to make a difference.
