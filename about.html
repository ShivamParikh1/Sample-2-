About Me

I’m Shivam Parikh, a Mechanical Engineering student at The Ohio State University with intended minors in Business and Computer Science, currently a junior by credit hours and on track to graduate in Spring 2028. I am passionate about engineering and technology, particularly aerospace and problem-solving, and I enjoy combining hands-on technical work with research to make meaningful contributions. I’ve gained experience through three research internships, where I developed skills in CAD, SolidWorks, MATLAB, and materials testing, while working on projects ranging from laser powder bed fusion to astrophysics analysis. Outside of academics, I stay active through lifting, running, and reading, and I’m committed to community involvement, mentoring, and fostering curiosity both in myself and others. Check out my personal statement for more on what drives me, and my projects section to see the work I’ve contributed to so far.
