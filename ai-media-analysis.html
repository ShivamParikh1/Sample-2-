AI in Media: 2023 Content Analysis

This comprehensive analysis examines the portrayal of artificial intelligence in contemporary media through quantitative content analysis of 500+ articles from major publications. The study employs natural language processing techniques to identify trends in AI representation, public perception patterns, and ethical considerations in media reporting.

Key findings reveal significant correlations between media framing and public understanding of AI capabilities, with particular focus on ethical implications and workforce automation narratives. Methodology combines computational analysis with traditional content evaluation frameworks.
