AI in Media: 2023 Content Analysis

This study conducted a content analysis of the first 50 CNN articles from 2023 to examine media portrayal of AI, focusing on privacy concerns, bias, regulation, ethics, and employment impact. Using a mixed-method approach, articles were systematically coded and analyzed both quantitatively (frequency counts) and qualitatively (thematic insights).

Key findings revealed ethical AI development as the dominant theme (76%), followed by privacy (48%), employment impact (42%), regulation (32%), and bias (30%). The findings highlight media emphasis on ethical considerations, underscoring public discourse around responsible AI governance. The study provides insights into how mainstream media frames AI’s societal implications, with ethical concerns overshadowing other critical themes.
