AI in Media: 2023 Landscape Analysis

This rigorous academic examination evaluates the portrayal of artificial intelligence across 500+ media sources from 23 countries, employing natural language processing and sentiment analysis techniques. The study reveals significant correlations between media framing and public perception indices, particularly in coverage of ethical AI development and workforce automation narratives.

Methodology combines computational linguistics with traditional content analysis frameworks, establishing a novel metric for bias quantification in AI-related journalism. Research conducted under peer review supervision with adherence to ACM ethical guidelines.
