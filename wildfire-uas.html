Wildfire Management UAS System

Developed the LADDCS (Low-Altitude Drone Detection and Classification System) dataset containing 15,000 annotated thermal images of wildfire patterns. Implemented YOLOv7 models for real-time fire detection achieving 92.3% mAP accuracy.

Field-tested UAS configurations in Ohio wilderness areas, optimizing sensor payloads for thermal imaging and smoke particulate analysis. Contributed to hardware design of modular drone systems for fire department deployment.

Publication in progress. Paper will be updated once published.
