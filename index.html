About me

Engineering curiosity into precision — from the lab bench to the launch pad.

I'm Shivam Parikh, a Mechanical Engineering student at The Ohio State University with intended minors in Business and Computer Science. I'm a senior by credit hours, on track to graduate in May 2028, with a 4.00 GPA.

I'm passionate about aerospace and hands-on problem-solving, combining lab research with real engineering builds. My research background spans laser powder bed fusion of high-strength steels and autonomous UAS systems for wildfire response, while my project work covers everything from quadcopter design to gearbox engineering and Arduino-based controls.

Outside of academics, I'm a member of the Buckeye Space Launch Initiative, and I stay active through weightlifting, running, tennis, and chess. I'm fluent in Hindi and Gujarati alongside English.
