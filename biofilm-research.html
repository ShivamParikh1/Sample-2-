Biofilm Detection Research

Conducted as part of an AP with WE Service project, this initiative focuses on studying how microgravity affects biofilm formation in space. Key aspects include:

  • Analyzing biofilm behavior in space environments and its potential risks to astronaut safety
  • Utilizing open-source NASA data to examine microbial growth patterns in microgravity
  • Developing algorithms for comparative analysis of biofilm growth in space versus Earth’s gravity
  • Developing algorithms for comparative analysis of biofilm growth in space versus Earth’s gravity

The goal of this is to apply computer science and engineering to solve real-world challenges in space exploration.
