Biofilm Detection Research

Conducted at Star Labs (OSU), this project focuses on developing non-destructive reflectance spectroscopy methods to detect biofilm growth in hydroponic systems. Key aspects include:

  • Microgravity simulation using clinostat rotation devices
  • Spectral analysis of Pseudomonas aeruginosa biofilms
  • Comparative Earth vs space environment data collection

Collaborating with NASA-affiliated researchers to establish early detection protocols for spacecraft hydroponic systems.
