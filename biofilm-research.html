Biofilm Detection in Microgravity

This NASA-affiliated research investigates reflectance spectroscopy as a non-destructive method for early biofilm detection in hydroponic systems. Developing protocols for microgravity simulation using clinostat rotation devices to study biofilm formation patterns in space-analog environments.

Experimental design includes spectral analysis of Pseudomonas aeruginosa biofilms at various growth stages, with comparative Earth vs simulated microgravity environment data.
